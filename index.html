A few facts about the world we live in




Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the largest of the terrain planets. Unlike the other planets in the solar system that are named after classical deities the Earth's name comes from the Angelo-Saxon word erda which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.
